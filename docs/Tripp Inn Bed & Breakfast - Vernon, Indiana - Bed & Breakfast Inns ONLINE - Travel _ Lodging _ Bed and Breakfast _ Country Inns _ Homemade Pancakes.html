Homemade Pancakes
1 1/4 cups sifted all purpose flour
2 1/2 tsps double acting baking powder
2 Tbsps sugar
3/4 tsp salt
1 egg
1 1/4 cup milk
3 Tbsps melted butter, margarine, fat or salad oil

Set griddle over low heat to warm up. Into medium bowl or wide mouthed pitcher sift flour, baking powder, sugar and salt. In small bowl beat eggs well; add milk and butter. Slowly stir into flour mixture, mixing only until dry ingredients are wet.

Cook in large skillet, 3 small pancakes.

When hot enough to make a drop of cold water dance, spray with Pam.

Drop batter from pitcher onto griddle, lightly spreading each cake with back of spoon into round about 4" in diameter.

Don't crowd cakes.

Cook over low heat until rim of each cake is full of broken bubbles and underside is golden brown.

Turn only once. Remove to heated platter. *(Add blueberries, unthawed or fresh 1 tsp, before turning).
